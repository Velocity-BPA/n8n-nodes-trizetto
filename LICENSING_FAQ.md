# Licensing FAQ

## General Questions

### What license does this software use?
This software is licensed under the Business Source License 1.1 (BSL 1.1). The BSL allows free use for personal, educational, research, and internal business purposes. Commercial use requires a separate commercial license from Velocity BPA.

### When does the license change?
On January 1, 2030, the license will automatically convert to Apache License 2.0, making it fully open source.

## Commercial Use Questions

### Is internal SaaS infrastructure considered internal use?
No. If the infrastructure supports a revenue-generating service, it is considered commercial use and requires a commercial license.

### What if customers never see the node?
Visibility to end users is irrelevant. If the node enables or supports revenue generation, it constitutes commercial use.

### What if we are a startup?
Startups are commercial entities. If you're using this node to support a revenue-generating product or service, you need a commercial license. We offer startup-friendly pricing tiers.

### What if we only use one workflow?
The scope of usage does not change license requirements. Even a single workflow in production for commercial purposes requires a license.

### Do proof-of-concepts require a license?
Internal, non-customer-facing POCs are permitted without a commercial license. However, once a POC moves to production for commercial use, a license is required.

### What about consulting or professional services?
If you're using this node as part of client-facing consulting or managed services engagements, a commercial license is required.

## Healthcare Industry Questions

### Are HIPAA-covered entities required to have a commercial license?
HIPAA compliance requirements don't automatically trigger commercial licensing. However, most healthcare organizations using this for production workloads will need a commercial license based on the commercial use criteria.

### Can healthcare organizations use this for internal automation?
Yes, internal automation that doesn't generate direct or indirect revenue is permitted. However, most healthcare IT deployments involve some form of revenue cycle management or billing operations that would qualify as commercial use.

## Technical Questions

### Does this node include telemetry or enforcement mechanisms?
No. This software includes no DRM, no telemetry, and no call-home functionality. Licensing is based on the honor system with informational notices only.

### Are runtime warnings blocking?
No. All licensing notices are informational and non-blocking. The software functions normally regardless of licensing status.
